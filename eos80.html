Returns:
adtgarray_like

References

Fofonoff, P. and Millard, R.C. Jr UNESCO 1983. Algorithms for computation of fundamental properties of seawater. UNESCO Tech. Pap. in Mar. Sci., No. 44, 53 pp. https://unesdoc.unesco.org/ark:/48223/pf0000059832_eng
